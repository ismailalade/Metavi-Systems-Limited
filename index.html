About Metavi Systems Limited

Metavi Systems Limited, operating in all major segments: Offshore | Oil and Gas, Merchant | Cargo, Passengers | Cruise, Governmental, Wind and Aquaculture – the company owns a network of 5 sales and service hubs all over the world. This ensures fast and professional on-site support for customers in the maritime industries.

Our Services

Metavi Systems Limited’s service portfolio spans services from inspection and maintenance to spare parts and repair, refurbishment and upgrades, hook replacement, agreements, and training. The global service network includes locations in Europe, Asia, the Americas, the Middle East, and Africa.
